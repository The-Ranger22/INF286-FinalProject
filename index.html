Welcome to CHICKS & CHUCKS official website

Here are some FUN FACTS about our foundation:

* A promise made to GOD during her battle with breast cancer, Cathy Youtsey Halloran and her devoted, caring and compassionate sister, Cheryl Youtsey Hardin. Both life long Campbell County Kentucky residents promised to do 3 things everyday since HE would restore her health – give HIM the glory, witness of HIS love to others and be a resource for other local breast cancer warriors.

* Celebrating our 12th year of being a local non-profit

* Chicks & Chucks was hatched on July 18, 2001

* Staffed by ALL Volunteers

* The Chick-line to call is 513 841-6756 for assistance.

Chicks & Chucks mission is to provide support and assistance to local breast cancer patients and their loved ones. Various types of FUNdraising and generous donations from Chicks & Chucks in our local communities enable Chicks & Chucks to be that resource.

Love,
