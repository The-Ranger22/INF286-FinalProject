Donations



Donations are used for purchasing wigs for breast cancer patients and for purchasing an entire year’s supply of resource books that are used by the Nurse Navigators, which they give to each newly diagnosed patient regardless of their treatment regimen -i.e. chemo, radiation, mastectomy or lumpectomy.

These are wonderful handbooks / resources for the patient and are very expensive. We are so excited to be able to earmark from our budget each year with enough money to purchase these valuable books. Monies have also been used for purchasing bras, prosthetics and numerous wigs.

Donations can now be made online to Chicks and Chucks by clicking on the “PayPal” button below. Chicks and Chucks uses the certified and secure PayPal system to process online transactions.


You DO NOT need a PayPal account to make a donation, just a valid credit card.
